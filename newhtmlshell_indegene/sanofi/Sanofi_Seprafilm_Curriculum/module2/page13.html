Impact on the Surgeon
A patient with ASBO can be an emergency patient
Conservative nonsurgical approaches lead to discomfort and a longer hospital stay
Approximately 20% of the patients are readmitted within 2 years for an additional open abdominal procedure complicated by adhesions
About 1 in 6 colorectal resection patients are readmitted within 2 years for intestinal obstruction and will be treated surgically
About 36.5% of abdominal adhesion-related readmission were due to SBO
Post-approval study showed that Seprafilm®
Has no impact on the incidence of SBO
Reduces the incidence of adhesive SBO requiring reoperation
Has no impact on the incidence of adhesive SBO requiring reoperation in patients with existing bowel adhesions
