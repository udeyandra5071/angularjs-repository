Neuroendocrine neoplasms (NENs) are tumours that originate from neuroendocrine cells located in various parts of the body.
They are a diverse group of malignant tumours with some common features; Express neuroendocrine makers.
However, not all exhibit hypersecretion of peptide hormones and related symptoms.
