Abdominal Postsurgical Adhesions
Incidence rate: 67% to 93%
Results of a 10-year follow-up study conducted in 12,584 patients who underwent lower abdominal surgery
7.3% of all readmissions were directly related to adhesions
25.4% of readmissions were due to a potential adhesion-related disorder during the first postoperative year
Incidence in Pelvic Procedures
Incidence of intraperitoneal postsurgical adhesions following pelvic surgery in women has been reported to be between 55% and 100%
Most common site of adhesion formation is the ovaries
About 20% of the patients are readmitted within 1 year for an additional open gynecologic procedure complicated by adhesions
Presence and severity of adhesions increase with each subsequent cesarean delivery
