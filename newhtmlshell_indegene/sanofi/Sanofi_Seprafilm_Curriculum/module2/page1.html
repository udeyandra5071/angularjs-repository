Module 2, Pathophysiology and Complications of Adhesions, discusses the mechanism of adhesion formation and its impact after abdominal surgery, obstetric surgery, and gynecology procedures.

Learning Objectives
  • After completing this module, you should be able to:
  • Describe the incidence, etiology, and the stepwise process of adhesion formation.
  • Discuss the pathophysiology of adhesion formation.
  • Explain why the omentum is not a barrier against adhesion formation.
  • Discuss the complications of adhesions after abdominal surgery, obstetric surgery, and gynecology procedures.
