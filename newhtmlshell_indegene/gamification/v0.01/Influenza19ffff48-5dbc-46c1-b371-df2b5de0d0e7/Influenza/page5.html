What are the guideline recommendations regarding nutrition ?
RECOMMENDED
  • Follow an individualized MNT program, preferably provided by registered dietitian
  • Can have whole grains, vegetables, fruits, legumes, and daily products, with an emphasis on foods higher in fiber and lower in gylcemic load
  • Choose healthy food and control portion
  • Can have foods in long-chain omega-3 fatty acids, mono-unsaturated fats such as fatty fish(EPA and DHA) and nuts and seeds(ALA)
DISCOURAGED
  • Avoid sugar sweetend beverages
  • Avoid protien rich foods
  • Take no more than one to two drinks per day(no more than one drink for adult women and no more than two drinks for adult men)
