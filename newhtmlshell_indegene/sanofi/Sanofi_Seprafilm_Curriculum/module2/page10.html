Complications of Postsurgical Adhesions
Seprafilm® Adhesion Barrier is indicated for use in patients undergoing abdominal or pelvic laparotomy as an adjunct intended to reduce the incidence, extent, and severity of postoperative adhesions between the abdominal wall and the underlying viscera such as omentum, small bowel, bladder, and stomach, and between the uterus and surrounding structures such as tubes and ovaries, large bowel, and bladder.8 Seprafilm does not reduce or prevent adhesion-related complications.8
Seprafilm® has not been demonstrated to reduce or prevent any pelvic disorders other than formation of post-operative adhesions.
